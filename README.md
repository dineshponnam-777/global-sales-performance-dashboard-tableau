# 🌍 Global Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

The Global Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project designed to analyze sales, profitability, customer segments, and regional performance across global markets.

This dashboard provides business stakeholders with a comprehensive view of key performance indicators (KPIs), sales trends, customer behavior, and geographical performance to support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Track profitability metrics
- Analyze regional sales distribution
- Understand customer segment contribution
- Identify sales trends over time
- Evaluate country-level performance globally

---

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### 1️⃣ Sales by Region

A horizontal bar chart comparing sales performance across major regions.

**Insights**
- North America generated the highest sales.
- Asia Pacific recorded the second-highest sales.
- South America showed strong sales contribution.
- Europe generated the lowest sales among analyzed regions.

---

### 2️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend

A line chart illustrating monthly sales performance.

**Insights**
- Sales fluctuated throughout the year.
- Strong recovery observed after low-performing months.
- Year-end months showed positive momentum.

---

### 3️⃣ Segment Distribution

A pie chart displaying sales contribution by customer segment.

**Segments**
- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

**Insights**
- Consumer segment contributes the largest share.
- Revenue distribution remains balanced across segments.

---

### 4️⃣ Global Sales Performance by Country

An interactive map visualizing country-level sales performance.

**Insights**
- North America remains a major revenue contributor.
- Asia-Pacific markets demonstrate strong growth potential.
- Sales are distributed across multiple international markets.
